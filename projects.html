A Club for Change


We are a club made up of students and staff passionate about changing the ways of the school community and local population for the better.


Our Projects

  • Thrift Store & Home Supplies

    Aims to address the waste generated on campus, particularly clothing and home/office supplies. By organizing a thrift store and redistributing items within the DKU community, it will help in reducing waste and promoting sustainability.

  • One Step Forward

    Aims to to provide students with foundational knowledge to start building their future path by hosting Alumni Sessions, organizing Industry Experts Talks and Workshops, and creating a platform with internships, startups, and job opportunities.

  • Waste Sorting

    Aims to raise awareness about effective waste management by organizing fun and friendly activities, including competitions, lectures, and information sessions, and encouraging the DKU community to take action!

  • Sustainable Photos

    The project aims to create a gallery and a sustainability guide for the DKU community. We want to highlight existing sustainable and green initiatives in Kunshan, Shanghai, and the DKU campus to foster environmental responsibility.

  • Film Screening

    By organizing a film screening, this initiative will portray a sustainability-related film, host a community cinematic experience, and promote sustainability.

  • Sustainable Ambassadors Program
