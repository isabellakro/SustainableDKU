About Us


Sustainable DKU was founded with the goal of making Duke Kunshan a sustainable, more environmentally friendly university. Inspired by the Sustainable Duke initiatives, Sustainable DKU hopes to drive sustainable missions across campus and encourage student participation in promoting such practices. Every academic year, we create new projects and welcome new members in our quest for a carbon-friendly campus. We meet as a group every two weeks, and each project holds weekly meetings to work to achieve our goals.
